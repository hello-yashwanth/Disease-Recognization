What is Disease Recognition System?

The Disease Recognition and Test Recommendation System is an artificial intelligence based healthcare assistant that analyzes symptoms entered by users and predicts possible diseases using machine learning models.

The system evaluates patterns in symptom data and identifies the most likely disease. After prediction, it recommends appropriate medical tests such as blood tests, ECG, X-rays or other diagnostics to assist doctors in confirming the diagnosis.

The platform also stores prediction history, generates downloadable medical reports, and provides an admin dashboard where diseases and symptoms can be managed to continuously improve the model.
